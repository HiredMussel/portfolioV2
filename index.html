About Me

Alex Musselwhite

I'm a programmer based in Bath. After obtaining a masters in Mathematics, I decided that the parts of the degree I'd enjoyed the most were the modules involving programming so I'd try and get a career in it. I applied for Mayden Academy later that year so that I could develop my professional and team-working skills before starting to apply for jobs.

I am a certified scrum master and have completed several projects working on both front- and back-end while at Mayden Academy. I also have some experience working on an IT Service Desk, so I'm accustomed to explaining technical ideas in simple language and interacting with customers.

I spend my free time with my cat and dog, tinkering with my PC, or running D&D games for some friends from Uni.
